Our Mission

There are still millions of Americans with out an affordable subscription to high speed internet. We are looking to solve this problem one community at a time.

Our Plan

First

The first step is to set up shop and start providing internet to customers so we can prove our ability.

We are looking to start in neighborhoods around the Bay Area.

Second

As our network grows, we'll stay true to our mission and start working with municipal governments who are looking to invest in internet infrastructure, as well as grow our service in underserved areas.

Third

Once we have scale, we would like to bring gigabit internet to every one of our customers.
